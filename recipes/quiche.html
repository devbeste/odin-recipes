Broccoli and Brie Quiche

Description

This savory quiche is packed with broccoli florets, eggs, and Brie cheese, making it perfect for a quick and easy dinner or a light lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 (17.5 ounce) package frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 cups chopped fresh broccoli
  • 1Â½ cups heavy cream
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 (8 ounce) round Brie cheese, sliced

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch pie dish with cooking spray.
  2. Step 2: Line the prepared pie dish with puff pastry, covering base and sides. Trim excess pastry. Brush bottom with Dijon mustard.
  3. Step 3: Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook 1 minute more. Mix in broccoli; cook 3 more minutes.
  4. Step 4: Whisk heavy cream, eggs, salt, nutmeg, black pepper, and Italian seasoning in a bowl.
  5. Step 5: Layer broccoli mixture over the puff pastry and top with Brie cheese slices. Pour in egg mixture.
  6. Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven until set and golden, about 30 minutes.
